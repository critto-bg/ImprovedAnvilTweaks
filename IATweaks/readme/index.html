What does this mod contain?

IA Tweaks is a set of various patches for Improved Anvil. It was made for the players who wish to ease, simplify or otherwise customize their experience with IA. The contents of this mod are split into following sections:

  1. Quality of life: these tweaks make your life generally easier and allow you to focus on the core experience of IA.
    • Happy NPC patch: removes reputation restrictions for having companions of different alignments in the same party.
    • Identify all items: what it says on the tin. You don't need to bother with the Identify spell and Lore anymore.
    • Simplifed economy: loot is converted to gold, now you spend less time shopping.
    • Disable hardened spellhold: if you don't want to experience the tougher version of the dungeon with the protagonist who went to Chapter 4 with too much experience.
  2. Custom kits: choose a custom class/kit combo for some of the existing joinable NPCs.
  3. Bulian's Tweaks & Miscellanea: tweaks aimed at IA veterans who want to experience as much content as possible in one playthrough and see the toughest versions of the improved encounters.

Installation

The installation procedure is fairly straightforward. Just unpack the mod into your BG2:EE 2.5 folder and run the WeiDU installer. The mod will ask you about every component it contains. If you don't have Improved Anvil installed already, the mod will fail, for obvious reasons. If you're updating to a newer version of the mod, make sure that you uninstall the previous one first and completely replace the mod's folder before proceeding.

Version history

Version 1.0
  1. The initial release of the mod.
  2. The following extra components previously found in the main Improved Anvil package are added: Reputation patch, Identify All Items.
  3. Simplified Economy, a brand new quality of life component, is added.
  4. Custom Kits, a brand new component that allows players to choose different starting classes and kits for some of the joinable companions, is added.
  5. Bulian's Tweaks & Miscellanea, a personal collection of tweaks to the main mod by Bulian aimed at relaxing certain restrictions on the content available to specific protagonists, is added.
